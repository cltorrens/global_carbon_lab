



















--- Data status codes ---

Data level codes:
	R: Raw (directly from sensor/logger)
	O: Outliers/anomalies removed
	G: Gaps imputed
	C: Corrected for drift (due to biofouling, calibration, etc.)
	D: Derived from other variables
	-: No information
